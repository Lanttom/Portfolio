my education

2015-2019

Brevet

In 2015, I started middle school, which lasted for four years. I attended the private school, Saint-Joseph, located in Libourne. During those four years, I joined a European Spanish class which gave me a certain level of fluency in the language. The final year of middle school ended with evaluations to obtain the Brevet des collèges, which I passed with flying colors, allowing me to enter high school without much apprehension.

2019-2022

Bac

In 2019, after obtaining my Brevet des collèges, I started high school. I attended the private school Montesquieu, located in Libourne. This period lasted for three years, during which we prepared for a final exam, the Baccalauréat, which varied depending on the chosen specialties. For my part, I chose the following specialties: NSI, HGGSP, and SVT. Consequently, during the final year of high school, I obtained my Baccalauréat.

2022-2027

EPSI

In 2022, after obtaining my Baccalauréat, I started my higher education studies at EPSI, a school specialized in computer science. This period will last for five years, during which I will prepare for a Bachelor's degree in System Operations Conceptualization and Integration, which is a RNCP level 6 qualification titled "Application Developer and Designer". During the first two years, I will have the option to specialize in BTS SIO SLAM.

2027

EPSI

In 2027, I hope to obtain my diploma at the end of the year and find a company where I will be able to apply all the knowledge I have acquired during the last five years.
